What is Basic Strategy?

Basic strategy is a mathematically proven set of rules that tells you the optimal play for every possible blackjack hand. By following basic strategy perfectly, you can reduce the house edge to as low as 0.5% - making blackjack one of the best games in the casino.

üí° Key Takeaway

Basic strategy is not about winning every hand - it's about making the mathematically correct decision that minimizes your losses over time.

Key Strategy Tips

üéØ Always Stand on 17+

Never hit a hard 17 or higher, regardless of what the dealer shows. The probability of busting is too high.

üíé Always Split Aces and 8s

Aces should always be split, and 8s should always be split because a 16 is a terrible hand.

üí∞ Double Down on 11

Always double down on 11 unless the dealer shows an Ace (then hit to avoid dealer blackjack).

üö´ Never Split 10s

A 20 is a great hand - don't split it into two potentially weaker hands.
